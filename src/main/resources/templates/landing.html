Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ChatApp.

What makes ChatApp secure?

ChatApp uses end-to-end encryption to ensure that your messages and files are protected and accessible only to you and your intended recipients.

Can I use ChatApp on multiple devices?

Yes, ChatApp supports cross-platform synchronization, allowing you to seamlessly switch between your desktop, tablet, and mobile devices while keeping all your conversations in sync.

Is there a free plan available?

Absolutely! ChatApp offers a free tier that includes essential features for small teams. You can upgrade to our Pro or Enterprise plans for additional functionalities and support.

How do I integrate ChatApp with other tools?

ChatApp provides easy integration options with various third-party tools through our API. You can customize your workflow by connecting ChatApp with your favorite applications like Slack, Google Drive, and more.

What support options are available?

We offer comprehensive support through our Help Center, documentation, and community forums. For Pro and Enterprise users, we provide priority support to ensure your team runs smoothly.
